Why Learn with Me?

Why Choose Me? Safety, Safety, Safety. Not only do I focus on teaching you the skills to make you an accomplished scuba diver, I continually stress safety. Not only in my classes as you learn, but also to prepare you to safely dive on your own without an instructor. Whether it is dealing with an equipment malfunction, changing water conditions, or your pre-dive preparation I will continuously work to ensure that you understand the inherent risks involved in scuba diving and help prepare you to be able to respond in a manner to overcome challenges. Starting with pre-dive matters such as staying in shape, hydrated, and well rested before your dive I will explain not only what you should be doing, but also the why you should take certain precautions. We will drill on common equipment failures such as recovering a regulator that has slipped from your mouth, replacing your mask if it is knocked off, and what to do if you or your buddy were to run out of air (I hope my training will ensure you don't run into this problem, but it's important to know what to do if you do). I will ensure you understand how your body reacts to the pressures of depth, and why certain steps, such as a safety stop is so important. What to look for in selecting a dive charter, or in conducting a shore dive. I keep my classes small so that I can give each student individual attention, and I will never rush a student to complete a class. I will take all the time necessary so that my students are best prepared to enjoy their diving experience. Diving is my passion, not my business. I'm not an instructor because I want to get rich, I'm an instructor because I want to share my love of diving with my students.and I get great satisfaction when one of my students gets to experience the freedom and beauty of safely entering the water and enjoy the beauty of the deep.
